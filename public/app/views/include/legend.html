Navigation

You can navigate through the elements in the panels in two ways:

  1. By clicking on a element name with your mouse
  2. By using the arrow keys on your keyboard
Editing

When an element field does not contain a value, you will see an input field which will be automatically in focus. You enter a value with your keyboard and then complete the action in one of two ways:

  1. click on "Yes" with your mouse
  2. Or with the keyboard:
    • regular text input: press [ENTER]
    • textarea (for longer descriptions) press [TAB] and then [ENTER]
